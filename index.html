About me

I am a PhD student in the Computational and Applied Mathematics and Operations Research (CMOR) department at Rice University since August 2023, working under the supervision of Professor Illya V. Hicks.

My research focuses on linear and integer programming formulations for optimization problems in graphs, and for approximating polynomial and trigonometric constraints, for instance in the Optimal Power Flow problem. I'm also interested in combinatorial optimization, complexity, machine learning and randomized algorithms.
